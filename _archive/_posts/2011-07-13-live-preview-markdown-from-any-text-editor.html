A clever little Mac app that monitors your Markdown file, as you work on it in real-time, and gives you an instant preview of it.

We are now writing all of NPMap's help documentation (which still needs a ton of work, so don't hate!) in Markdown, so I'm sure I'll be using this app several times a day.

It's just $2.99 in the Mac App Store.
