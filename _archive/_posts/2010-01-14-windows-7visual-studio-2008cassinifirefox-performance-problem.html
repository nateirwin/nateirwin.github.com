It worked for me! http://joshclose.net/?tag=cassini EDIT: The link above is down at the moment. Here are the instructions:
  1. Open Firefox and type "about:config" in the address bar.
  2. Use the textbox at the top to filter on "v6."
  3. Double-click on the "ipv6" setting.
