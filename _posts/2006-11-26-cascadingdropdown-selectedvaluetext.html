This caused problems when trying to access the SelectedValue. The only way to quasi-cleanly work around this was to manually edit the associated javascript. Well, I just found out that this problem was addressed in the latest release. You can now access the SelectedValue of a CascadingDropDown control without having to implement a messy workaround: 
ddlCars.SelectedItem.Text
