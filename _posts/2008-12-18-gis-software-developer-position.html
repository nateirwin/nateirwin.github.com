There’s an opening for a GIS Software Developer position in Fort Collins, Colorado. I’m familiar with this position, and know that it involves working on some pretty interesting projects. Here are the details:

Colorado State University
Fort Collins, CO 80523-1490

GIS Software Developer position (Research Associate III Special) available with the Center for Environmental Management of Military Lands, to be assigned at the National Park Service in Fort Collins, Colorado. Position is contingent on the availability of funds.

The National Park Service (NPS) is looking for an experienced GIS Software Developer to design and develop web-based GIS solutions using primarily ESRI technologies in a Microsoft .NET environment. Developer will participate in the transformation of applications to a SOA framework. Developer will be a key member of the small, agile development team comprised of intelligent, motivated technical professionals. Excellent opportunity to develop and apply a variety of cutting edge technologies.

The NPS program in Ft. Collins, CO. performs inventory and monitoring in national parks throughout the U.S. Developer support for the program is obtained through an agreement with Colorado State University. Work will be on-site in Ft. Collins, population 130,000, which has received numerous national awards and recognitions as one of the best places to live.

MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS:

  • Bachelor's degree in GIS, Computer Science or related field.
  • Three years demonstrated experience in web-based and desktop GIS application development.
  • Experience as GIS developer for enterprise-level GIS applications.
  • Experience using ArcGIS Server, ArcGIS Desktop, ArcSDE, ArcIMS and ArcInfo.
  • Application development experience with SQL Server, XML and C# in a .NET environment.

DESIRABLE QUALIFICATIONS (Not Required):

  • Experience working in remote sensing application development.
  • Experience with service oriented architecture (SOA).
  • Experience devising WSDL and SOAP specifications.
  • Experience working in an agile development environment.
  • Experience in enterprise database development with SQL Server or Oracle.

RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Design, develop, and deploy customized web-GIS solutions and services using ArcGIS Server, ArcGIS Desktop, ArcSDE, and SQL Server.
  • Develop custom viewers, tools, databases and services in a .NET environment using C#, XML and SQL.
  • Participate in transformation of existing spatial applications to a SOA framework.
  • Provide user support, training and technical communication to multiple audiences.
  • Work closely with business analysts to design GIS services that meet functional requirements.
