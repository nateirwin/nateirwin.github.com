--- layout: post title: Update to reeder includes Readability support published: true meta: _edit_last: "2" _wp_old_slug: "" tags: - Technology type: post status: publish --- Looking for another reason to buy an iPhone? The app that gets, by far, the most use on my iPhone is reeder, an RSS reader application that syncs seamlessly with Google Reader. This is the most elegant app on my phone, and it added a huge feature today: Readability support. Readability basically takes a blog post, extracts the text, and presents it in a highly-readable format. It gets rid of ads, reformats the text, and concatenates "pages" together. I highly, HIGHLY recommend reeder, and if you have a Mac, you should also check out the public beta of the desktop version.
