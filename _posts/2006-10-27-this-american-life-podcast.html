I caught this one from Lifehacker. Up until now, NPR's wonderfully intelligent show, This American Life, was only available on streaming connections or via Audible.com - for a fee, of course. Now the most recent show is being released weekly as a podcast. To get to archived shows, you still have to pay $.95 per show to Audible.com or iTunes. This is great news! I wish that I had the time to sit down every week, at the same time, and listen to This American Life on my local NPR station (KUNC) - but I simply don't. Now I'll be able to listen whenever I have the time and inclination. Link to This American Life podcasts
